What Makes a Coding Witch

A coding witch is an individual who identifies as a witch in her mundane life, and integrates the same logic, morality, and laws to her coding work as she does her physical workings.

Types of Coding Witches

A traditional coding witch is someone who abides by older traditions and laws within her coding fields. She respects the history that led to the development of her craft, and honors it within her coding style.

A kitchen coding witch is someone who likes to creatively invent, test, and integrate many types of coding styles and rules, with the goal of making something different as a whole out of separate parts.

A hedge coding witch is someone who avidly seeks out mentorship and apprenticeship both with modern programmers, as well as programmers past. She utilizes lessons from all she interacts with to fine tune her craft.

An elemental coding witch is someone who partakes in projects that requires certain structure, or ritual, if you will. She thrives on using familiar elements in her coding consistently.

A green coding witch is someone who adjusts to the environment she is in. She is focused on bringing equity, balance, and completion to her projects.

A solitary coding witch is someone who thrives working and learning on her own. She avoids typical academic pathways and rarely works in a teamwork setting.

A secular coding witch is someone who is eager to learn all different types of coding and programming. She does not adhere to a particular school of thought, and, instead, focuses on a wide breadth of knowledge.

An ecelectic coding witch is someone who thrives on chaos. She made adhere or borrow from any of the above types.

Spirituality

Spirituality in a witches coding work is a concept that may seem strange to apply to something that thrives on structure, code, formulas, and logic. For a coding witch, it is important to balance the structure/individualism with the world around her. She seeks to apply justice in ways that are often overlooked. In any culture, systems still reflect the eniviroment around them. A coding witch keeps this in mind, even as she implements that which would be considered scientific.
